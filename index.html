About Us

Mathibele Mahlaela Attorneys Inc. is a South African law firm situated in Centurion, Pretoria. We are dedicated to providing diverse and professional legal services. Our results-driven approach is focused on delivering effective legal solutions tailored to our clients' needs.

With years of experience in various fields of law such as labour law, commercial law, company law, corporate law, estate planning and administration of estates, etc., we pride ourselves on our commitment to integrity, honesty and loyalty, and unwavering commitment to client satisfaction. Our team of legal professionals provides tailored, solution-oriented services aimed at securing the best possible outcomes for our clients.
